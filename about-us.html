Who We Are

Our Story

SmartUchaguzi is a pioneering project developed by Group 4 at the University of Dodoma, Tanzania, as part of our Bachelor's Degree in Information Systems. Titled "SmartUchaguzi: Transparent and Secure Web-Based Election System at the University of Dodoma Elections," this initiative leverages cutting-edge technology to revolutionize student elections.

Our platform integrates a neural network model for real-time fraud detection and blockchain technology for secure vote storage, ensuring transparency, integrity, and accessibility in the electoral process. We started this project to address the challenges of traditional voting systems, such as fraud, inefficiency, and lack of trust, by providing a digital solution that empowers every voter.

Our Mission

We aim to create a secure, transparent, and user-friendly voting platform that empowers students at UDOM to participate in fair elections, setting a new standard for university governance. By combining advanced technology with a user-centric design, we strive to make voting accessible to all eligible members of the UDOM community, whether they are students voting for UDOSO or academic staff voting for UDOMASA.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every university election at UDOM is conducted with unparalleled transparency and efficiency. By expanding SmartUchaguzi, we hope to inspire other institutions to adopt secure, technology-driven voting systems, fostering trust and participation in democratic processes across Tanzania and beyond.
