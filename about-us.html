Our Story

SmartUchaguzi is a groundbreaking project by Group 4 at the University of Dodoma, Tanzania, as part of our Bachelor's Degree in Information Systems. Titled "SmartUchaguzi: Transparent and Secure Web-Based Election System at the University of Dodoma Elections," this initiative leverages cutting-edge technology to revolutionize student elections.

Backed by a neural network model for real-time fraud detection and blockchain technology for secure vote storage, our system ensures transparency, integrity, and accessibility in the electoral process.

Our Mission

We aim to create a secure, transparent, and user-friendly voting platform that empowers students at UDOM to participate in fair elections, setting a new standard for university governance.
